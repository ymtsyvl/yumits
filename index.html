INTERNATIONAL FOOD SUBSCRIPTION

80 Days offers a unique way to experience culture from across the globe, by combining cuisine and culture in a bi-monthly, expert-curated delivery box. We use a region of the world to inspire us, creating unique packages that make every delivery an occasion.

HOW IT WORKS

Every two months, we choose a unique and striking culture or region.

We source local ingredients to bring our ideas to life.

We deliver the 80 Days experience directly to your door.
